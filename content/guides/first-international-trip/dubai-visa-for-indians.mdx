Short answer: in almost all cases, **yes — you need to sort a visa before you fly.** Indian passport holders are not visa-free for the UAE, so for most people a tourist visa has to be arranged in advance.

There's one exception worth knowing, and a few numbers that are genuinely useful to plan around. We'll keep this honest: visa rules and fees move, so treat everything below as a realistic guide and confirm the specifics on the official source before you apply.

## The exception: visa on arrival

If you hold a valid visa or residence permit — usually one with at least six months left — from a set of countries including the US, UK, EU/Schengen, Australia, Canada, Japan, New Zealand, South Korea or Singapore, you may be eligible for a **visa on arrival** instead of a pre-arranged one.

The "may" is doing real work in that sentence. The eligible list and the conditions attached to it change, and your individual case can differ. So if you think this applies to you, don't take our word for it — check the official portal, where it's spelled out for your exact situation.

## How long it takes, and what it costs

For the standard route — applying online before you travel — here's the realistic shape, not a promise:

- **Standard processing:** commonly around 3 to 5 working days. It can stretch in the busy October–March season, and UAE offices are closed on weekends, so a Thursday application effectively waits for the new week.
- **Express:** roughly 24 to 48 hours for an extra fee, if you're cutting it fine.
- **Cost:** a 30-day tourist visa tends to land somewhere in the region of ₹6,700 to ₹9,500 depending on the type and who you apply through.

These are ranges on purpose. Anyone quoting you one exact, unchanging fee is guessing — the official portal and your airline's visa service will show the live number.

## What you'll need to apply

Most rejections are boring document problems, not real eligibility issues. Have these ready and clean:

- A clear scan of your passport's first and last pages
- A white-background photo to the required dimensions
- A confirmed return flight booking
- A confirmed hotel booking — yes, even if you're staying with family

If anything is blurry or the dates don't line up, fix it before you submit. It's the single easiest way to avoid a delay.

## When to start

Give it at least two to three weeks before you fly. If your trip is sooner than the standard processing window, that's the moment to look at express — or to gently move your date rather than gamble on it arriving.
